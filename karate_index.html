About Our Academy

Welcome to Karate Academy, where tradition meets excellence. Our dojo has been teaching the art of karate,kalari,silimbam,ninjack,locks,balance and more for over 20 years, producing champions and instilling discipline in students of all ages.

Our experienced senseis are dedicated to passing on the wisdom of karate, teaching not just physical techniques but also the philosophical principles that guide practitioners on their journey to self-improvement.

Our masters are certified by Soke KENYU MABUNI ( governer of Shito Ryu )
